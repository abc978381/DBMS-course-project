Elon Musk is the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX. He founded SpaceX in 2002. Tesla is headquartered in Austin, Texas. Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa. He later moved to Canada before settling in the United States.

Jeff Bezos founded Amazon in 1994. He served as CEO of Amazon for many years before stepping down. Amazon is headquartered in Seattle, Washington. Bezos also owns Blue Origin, a space exploration company. He acquired The Washington Post in 2013.

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Google and Alphabet. He joined Google in 2004. Google was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in 1998. The company is headquartered in Mountain View, California. Google developed the Android operating system.

Microsoft was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975. Satya Nadella is the current CEO of Microsoft. The company is based in Redmond, Washington. Microsoft acquired LinkedIn in 2016 for over 26 billion dollars. Gates established the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation to support global health initiatives.

Apple was co-founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in 1976. Tim Cook became the CEO of Apple after Jobs passed away in 2011. Apple is headquartered in Cupertino, California. The company launched the iPhone in 2007. Apple acquired Beats Electronics in 2014.
